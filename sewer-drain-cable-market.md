<p><p>The sewer drain cable market is categorized into two main types: inner core and no core cables. Inner core cables feature a robust metal or plastic core, providing added strength and durability for cutting through tough clogs and debris. In contrast, no core cables are more flexible and lightweight, making them ideal for navigating through complex plumbing systems. Both types cater to different needs in drain cleaning, allowing professionals to choose the appropriate cable based on the specific conditions of the plumbing system.</p></p>
